### Hi there, I'm Liz 👋

I am a Systems Engineer and a freshly minted Ph.D. graduate from the University of Virginia. I spend my time designing and evaluating AI systems for complex, high-stakes environments like healthcare. 

If you are looking at my public repositories and wondering where all the code went, don't worry. I haven't been staring at a blank screen for the last few years! Because my research heavily involves sensitive electronic health records and proprietary clinical models, my actual work is locked away behind strict HIPAA compliance and institutional firewalls. 

In the wild, my technical work bridges the gap between what AI systems look like on paper and how they actually behave when they hit the messy reality of clinical deployment. I focus heavily on algorithmic fairness, reinforcement learning, and making sure models are safe before they are set loose.

**🔧 Under the Hood:**
*   **Focus:** AI Evaluation, Systems Modeling, Causal Inference, and keeping models honest.
*   **The Human Element:** Cross-disciplinary translation. With my background in cognitive science and prior experience as a mental health therapist, I excel at bridging the communication gap between clinical domain experts, engineers, and product teams.
*   **Tools of the Trade:** Python, R, SQL, Data Visualization, and building scalable pipelines for messy real-world data.
